The NCN (NVIDIA Compute Network) project is developed under the NVIDIA ecosystem and aligned with its mission to advance global AI computing. It operates as an independent blockchain initiative powered by NVIDIA-inspired technologies.

NVAI is the native token that powers the NCN ecosystem. It serves as the primary medium for computation payments, staking, node rewards, and governance across the decentralized GPU compute network.

Proof of Compute is NCN's consensus mechanism where GPU nodes validate actual AI workloads ‚Äî such as training or inference ‚Äî instead of wasteful mining. Each task is cryptographically verified, ensuring that real computation contributes directly to model performance and network trust.

The NVAI Presale offers early participants the opportunity to acquire tokens before the public exchange listing. Funds raised during the presale are allocated to network development, GPU infrastructure expansion, and ecosystem incentives.

You can participate through the official NVAI Presale interface on this site. Simply connect your wallet (ETH, BNB, USDT, or USDC) and purchase NVAI tokens. Once the presale ends, your tokens will be claimable or automatically distributed to your connected address before the mainnet launch.

$NVAI gains value through multiple on-chain utilities:

Compute Payments: Used to pay for AI training and inference tasks.

Staking Rewards: Earned by GPU node operators and delegators.

Model Licensing & Marketplace Fees: Revenue from AI model sharing.

Deflationary Burns: A portion of compute fees are permanently burned to increase scarcity.
